About Me

Hi, I’m Estefan. Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I like to challenge myself by always having side projects or developing new skills. This web portfolio is a collection of my favorite projects so far, so please feel free to look around!

When I’m not doing engineering stuff, I like to play guitar and produce music on my laptop. I also love to go on adventures around town or neighboring states. I’ve been to New Hampshire and Maine a couple of time but I think’s it’s time I venture outside of the country.

In essence, I like adventuring, making music, and have a passion for innovative solutions. This last point is what lead me to Mechanical Engineering and all its ingenious designing. I began my engineering career as a Mechanical Engineering Intern at Bosch Thermotechnology where I got to work with a small team to develop the latest iteration of the IDS system. I learned how to work with a multidisciplined team as well gained a ton of technical experience using Siemens NX and Excel’s VBA. This was transferable to my second co-op experience with Sonos Inc, as the sole Mechanical-Materials Management System co-op where I led a project that sought to restructure the way materials were tracked by the company. I was able to gain a broader understanding outside of Engineering and gain an appreciation for supply chain and DevOps.

I am now searching for my third co-op experience and hope to get back into the action of Mechanical Engineering to apply all that I’ve learned and expand my knowledge in the process.
